Welcome to Grouper

Grouper is a practical way to communicate with the different people in your life.

What is Grouper?

Grouper is about groups. Each set of people in your life become a different group.

Is this another group chat app?

No. Groupers has never aimed to be a group chat app. While there is a messaging feature, as our layout suggest, we don't try to promote that as the primary functionality.
